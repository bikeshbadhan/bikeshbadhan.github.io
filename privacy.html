Privacy at Bikesh Inc.

Privacy Policy

At Bikesh Inc., the protection of your information is paramount, and we are fully committed to the responsible handling of any personal information that you choose to share. We restrict the collection and usage of your personal data solely for communication purposes with you, such as answering your queries or sending you the information you may have requested. Should you decide to provide personal information—such as by filling in a "Contact Us" form with your email address or postal address and submitting it through our website—the information will be used only for the purpose of responding to you and providing whatever information you may have requested. We neither collect personal information to use for any commercial marketing purposes nor do we create individual profiles for any type of commercial activity. All information provided by the user shall be utilized internally by Bikesh Inc. strictly for the aforementioned purposes. We hereby confirm that such information shall not, under any circumstances, be given to third parties except in cases where this is required either by force of law or when indispensably needed to protect the rights, property, or safety of Bikesh Inc., our users, or others. This policy exemplifies our continuing commitment to transparency, privacy, and responsible management of personal information.
